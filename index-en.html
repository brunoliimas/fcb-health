WHO IS FCB HEALTH BRASIL

The FCB Health Brasil is part of IPG Health, the largest and most awarded health marketing group in the world. A full service operation, prepared to meet the demands of healthcare brands wherever they are. Starting with yours.

The new normal has to be new. But it can also be extraordinaire.

Health is at the center of the global stage. Together we moved through a pandemic that has transformed our way of working, bonding, and living. And we experienced it all being as connected as ever. FCB Health wants to deep dive into this new reality together with you. Raising the bar of healthcare communications, leveraging creativity as a business accelerator, merging empathy and technology. And, if all goes well, creating innovative solutions that elicit the classic question – why didn’t somebody think of this before?

Healthier people. Healthier brands.

The healthcare frontiers are now more subtle, porous, and flexible than ever. And we like it this way. The health territory is increasingly more disputed by brands which, regardless of the markets they come from, strive to make a difference in the lives of consumers, patients, caretakers, HCPs, public and private decision makers. If that is your case, let’s definitely talk. Google processes over 1 billion health-related searches every day. And we’re sure you already have the answers for many of them.

A network that has won all imaginable awards. And one more: people living better lives.

FCB Health has redefined creativity in healthcare communications. If we only look at 2021, Cannes Lions and CLIO have both recognized FCB Health as the Healthcare Network of the Year. Diego and Mauro, who lead the Brazilian FCB Health office, are creatives by trade; they have won the first ever Pharma Gold Lion for Brazil and are regularly invited to be jury members at the major global advertising awards. It all comes with the greatest recognition possible: the feeling that our work contributes to the health journey of so many people.

Diego Freitas

Co-Managing Director, ECD

“ Diego started his carrier at a traditional print company in Brazil, where he discovered his love for design and visual arts. He then went on to study economics, but decided to give this up to blaze new trails. After a time in Madrid, he came to Lisbon, where he joined his first IPG Health agency. During his post-graduate studies at the Lisbon Ad School he worked as a freelancer in London and Lisbon. He then spent two years at digital and guerrilla agencies. Just before he was named one of the top 3 ADs in Portugal, he returned to Brazil as Country Manager for the FunnyHow? agency, working with brands such as Guinness, Desperados, Nintendo, Teva, Pfizer and Durex. In 2015, Diego took over the leadership of the creative department at Havas Life São Paulo, where he won gold and silver at Cannes, as well as two gold awards at Clio Health and awards from The One Show and Epica festivals. He was a jury member at the Cannes Lions in 2017. Along with Mauro, Diego was responsible for Parkinsounds, the only project ever to receive a Cannes award in both the Pharma and Entertainment categories.”

Mauro Arruda

Co-Managing Director, ECD

“ Mauro has a 20+ year-experience as a senior copywriter, creative director, CCO and CEO, leading communication projects for major health & wellness brands in Brazil and LATAM. Following a solid experience in Rx, OTC and consumer brands while running his own creative boutique in São Paulo, Mauro joined Havas Health in 2008 to co-found the Havas Life LATAM hub, where he acted as Managing Director and CCO until mid 2018. Among his creative recognitions are Cannes Lions, CLIO Health, LIAA, Epica and The One Show in areas such as pharma, mobile, digital, music and audio technique. Mauro was a member of the inaugural Lions Health jury in 2014 and a speaker at the 2016 edition. He has also been a jury member at the CLIOs and the Medical Marketing & Media Awards, and will be part of the Executive Jury at the Global Awards 2018. Along with Diego, he was responsible for the first gold lion ever deliverd to a LATAM IPG Health agency with the Parkinsounds.”

WHAT WE DO
